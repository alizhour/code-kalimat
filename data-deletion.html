Account Deletion Request - Code Kalimat

We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your Code Kalimat account and associated data, please follow the process outlined below.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request account deletion, please email us at:

sajed.atwe@gmail.com

In your email, please include:

  • Your email address associated with your Code Kalimat account
  • Your username (if applicable)
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request - Code Kalimat"
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account

What Data Will Be Deleted

The following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your user profile information
  • Account credentials and authentication data
  • App usage data and preferences
  • Any content you've created within the app
  • Your email address and contact information

Processing Time

Account deletion requests will be processed within 30 days of receiving your email. Once processed, your data cannot be recovered.

Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • Most data is deleted immediately from our active systems
  • Some data may remain in backups for up to 90 days for technical reasons
  • We may retain certain data if required by law
  • Anonymous analytics data that cannot be linked to you may be retained

Alternative Options

If you're having issues with the app or have concerns, you can also contact us to:

  • Temporarily deactivate your account
  • Modify your privacy settings
  • Export your data before deletion
